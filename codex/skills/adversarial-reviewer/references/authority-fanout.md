# Authority Fanout

Use authority packets to prevent root-reviewer overconfidence.

Authority lanes:

- evidence-authority: current grounding and reachability
- soundness-authority: witnesses, preservation, progress, illegal inhabitants, partial handlers
- invariant-scope-authority: owner, layer, direction, and scope
- hazard-footgun-authority: misuse chain and future incident risk
- complexity-remediation-authority: remediation breadth and complexity delta
- verification-authority: proof lane and evidence of absence
- finding-skeptic: strongest no-finding countercase

If custom agents are unavailable, the root reviewer may emit root-equivalent packets, but must label them as such.

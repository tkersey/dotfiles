# Grading rubrics

## Material finding threshold

A candidate becomes a material finding only when it is grounded, material, current, owned, remedy-shaped, verification-pathed, and cleared by authority.

## Non-finding threshold

Use Non-Finding Ledger when a concern is unsupported, stale, already fixed, wrong-owner, wrong-layer, out of scope, low-value, duplicate-boundary, proof-only, validate-first, or overbroad.

## Structural remediation threshold

Recommend structural remediation only when a narrow accretive fix cannot restore the invariant or proof surface. Name why smaller is insufficient.

## Validation-first threshold

Use `validate-first` when a candidate is plausible and material but mutation is not yet proven, and the validation result would change route, severity, or scope.

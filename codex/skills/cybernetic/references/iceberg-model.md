# Iceberg Model

Move from surface events to deeper causes.

```text
Events
Patterns
Structures
Mental models
```

## Questions

### Event

What happened?

### Pattern

What keeps happening over time?

### Structure

What rules, incentives, flows, delays, information gaps, and power relations produce the pattern?

### Mental model

What assumptions, goals, identities, or paradigms make the structure seem natural?

## Rule

Do not stop at event-level explanation unless the system is clear and local.

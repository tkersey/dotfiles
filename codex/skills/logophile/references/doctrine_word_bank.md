# Doctrine word bank

Use words that change **procedure**, not just tone.

## Type-theoretic / soundness-heavy
- unsound
- unwitnessed
- ill-typed
- illegal inhabitant
- partial
- incoherent
- non-compositional
- well-founded
- canonical

## Coding / review
- mechanistic
- accretive
- traceable
- adversarial
- invariant-preserving
- deterministic
- idempotent
- hermetic

## Research / writing
- source-disciplined
- calibrated
- falsifiable
- synthetic
- parsimonious

Avoid generic praise words like `thoughtful`, `insightful`, or `sophisticated` unless surrounding text makes them operational.

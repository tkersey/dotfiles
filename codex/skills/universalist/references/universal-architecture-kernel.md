# Universal Architecture Kernel

Universal architecture designs software around canonical boundary artifacts and, when process/change interaction, locality, indexing, or context action is semantic, explicit structure inside worlds and their two-dimensional, description, and generalized-morphism categories.

Central rule:

```text
Primitive operations may be arbitrary.
Composition boundaries may not be arbitrary.
Process composition and architecture-change composition may not be conflated.
Local context may not be implicit when it changes correctness.
Description composition may not be invented independently of indices.
Context framing may not be duplicated independently of action laws.
```

## Kernel artifacts

1. **Worlds and boundaries** — owned domains, embeddings, projections, handlers, interpreters, serializers, compilers, views, migrations, contextual frames, optics, and compatibility squares.
2. **Free syntax and coherent observations** — inspectable operations plus sanctioned projections and equivalence.
3. **Transported semantics, lifted implementations, free builders, and obstructions** — explicit movement, realization, or witnessed impossibility.
4. **Behavioral coalgebras and effects** — ongoing state/trace behavior plus operation signatures and handlers.
5. **Base composition geometry** — categories, monoidal/Freyd structures, operads, PROPs, traced and resource-sensitive worlds.
6. **Two-dimensional composition** — double categories separate horizontal processes/generalized arrows from vertical migrations/refinements/strict maps; typed squares, both pastings, and interchange make compatible local change compositional.
7. **Equipments and base change** — tight maps induce companions, conjoints, or restrictions of loose generalized arrows only with explicit laws and effectivity.
8. **Description composition** — pointwise, Day, promonoidal, substitutional, or monadic products for indexed descriptions.
9. **Context actions and Tambara modules** — generalized capabilities frame coherently through context actions.
10. **Comonadic spatiality** — points, patches, halos, germs, bases, situated objects, and continuous boundaries.
11. **Explicit IR and law tests** — callbacks, residuals, migrations, squares, and frames become data plus interpreter and executable witnesses.

## Operating test

A proposed artifact, square calculus, spatial structure, description product, or context action is legitimate only if it changes at least one of:

- code or module shape;
- ownership, authority, or construction paths;
- horizontal/vertical arrow representation;
- square construction, pasting, normalization, or invalidation;
- legal composition, state space, provenance, compatibility, or migration;
- index/resource/context/locality representation;
- interpreter/projection/handler/double-functor ownership;
- static analysis, tests, resources, or obstruction handling;
- ability to compose local migration/refactor witnesses into a global proof.

A category, 2-category, adapter plus one compatibility witness, PROP, DPO rewrite, product, pointwise map, ordinary profunctor, Reader parameter, residual record, dependency graph, or context object remains preferable when it already satisfies required observations without double-category, Day, Tambara, or spatial claims.

# Review-Closure Route Invalidation

Use `$negative-ledger` after a controller-proven realization/design failure, not for every review finding.

Capture route-family negative evidence when:

```text
same accepted class recurs after realization
design exceeds hard semantic surface
construct/law mapping fails
proof cannot cover the selected normal form
holdout falsifies a route family
```

Bind:

```text
campaign
AC/CEB/MBK fingerprints
counterexample class
failed design/route family
falsifying evidence
reopening criteria
```

Do not use the ledger to classify finding relevance; the acceptance contract and counterexample record own that.

A later campaign may reopen a route only when its recorded applicability or reopening condition changed.

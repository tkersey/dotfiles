# Plan Fresh-Eyes Press Pass

During press pass, reread the current final plan while treating prior convergence as untrusted.

Look for:
- contradictions;
- missing owners or due dates;
- untestable requirements;
- stale assumptions;
- rollback or abort gaps;
- scope creep;
- weak proof;
- implementation-order mistakes;
- sections that repeat rationale without changing execution behavior.

If any blocking `errors` remain after the pass, continue iterating instead of finalizing. If the pass finds no material delta, record the no-delta evidence in Convergence Evidence or Iteration Change Log.

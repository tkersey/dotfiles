Use these only as starting points. The task should drive the final selection.

## Anti-pattern words
Often weak unless the task specifically calls for them:
- thoughtful
- nuanced
- elegant
- sophisticated
- insightful
- comprehensive
- polished
- smart

Why they are weak:
- they bias tone more than procedure,
- they rarely imply a concrete failure check,
- they are often redundant with better words.

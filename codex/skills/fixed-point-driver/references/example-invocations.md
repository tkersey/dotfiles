Use $fixed-point-driver.

Inputs:
- current artifact_state_id
- active Resolution Warrants and Surface Budget Ledger

- consume warrants, not prose
- emit Truth Units, Warrant Intake / Parallelism Plan, Adversarial Action Ledger,
  Surface Delta Receipts, Fixed-Point Test, Closure Handoff, Final State, and Do Next

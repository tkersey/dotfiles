# FPS-v2 Workspace Slice

FPS-v2 extends the semantic realization slice with:

```text
workspace and plan sequence
branch epoch and base head
claim and fencing token
session/executor
external worktree
structured resources
```

These are not annotations. They are mutation authority.

A valid FPSR-v2 may be sealed into CS-v1 only while the claim/fencing and
workspace lineage remain current.

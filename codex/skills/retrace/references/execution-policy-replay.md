# Replaying EPG Decisions

Replay the visible evidence state, not hidden rationale.

Freeze:

```text
EPG policy ID/revision/digest
EPS state ID/digest and satisfied atoms
eligible/shielded actions
EPD selected rule/action
artifact/authority/frontier state
outcome-blind evidence available at selection
```

Counterfactual lanes may compare:

```text
historical selected action
best eligible alternative
information-first alternative
lower-risk/surface alternative
current-policy intervention
```

Keep historical source fact separate from replay consensus.

Do not expose outcome evidence to outcome-blind lanes.

Useful result:

```text
Would a different lawful action have reduced regret, rework, or surprise given what was knowable then?
```

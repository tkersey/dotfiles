# Resolve Material Improvement Closure

For `$resolve` closure, require:

- clean review streak;
- full validation;
- PR sweep;
- same-cluster stop rule closed or not triggered;
- negative route gate pass;
- material improvement score present.

Do not claim material improvement from packets alone. Check outcome fields:

- same_cluster_findings_after_first_fix
- production_net
- route_changed_by_exclusion
- cas_review_iterations
- churn_reduced

# Cybernetic Closure

For cybernetic interventions, closure is not only "tests passed."

## Closure packet

```yaml
cybernetic_closure:
  system_type: clear | complicated | complex | chaotic | mixed | unknown
  intervention_type: checklist | expert_analysis | safe_to_fail_probe | stabilize_first | redesign_feedback | change_rules | change_goal | handoff | blocked
  expected_feedback: "..."
  leading_indicators_checked: []
  lagging_indicators_deferred: []
  stop_conditions_recorded: []
  local_vs_whole_tradeoff_checked: yes | no
  closure_allowed: yes | no
```

## Rule

For complex systems, green tests are probe evidence, not full proof. Require monitoring or next feedback interval.

For chaotic systems, verify stabilization before optimization closure.

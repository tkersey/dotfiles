# Decision Evidence Model

Keep five layers separate:

```text
presence
activation
decision influence
contract compliance
downstream outcome
```

Evidence strength:

1. structured `SDR-v1`;
2. explicit assistant attribution;
3. contract-aligned action after activation;
4. associated downstream outcome;
5. co-occurrence/raw mention.

Only levels 1–2 support strong attribution.

A contract-consistent action may still have happened without the skill.

A successful outcome may have many causes.

# Positive Production Net Embargo

After same-cluster recurrence:

```text
production_net must be <= 0
```

unless the record proves one of:

- more semantic surface is retired/collapsed than added;
- illegal state is made uninhabitable at the right boundary;
- explicit user/upstream expansion acceptance exists;
- review-distillation/boundary redesign proves positive net is lowest total-system surface.

Blocked by default:

- another validation branch;
- another evidence predicate;
- another compatibility/fallback case;
- another one-wound production path.

# Negative-Ledger Ratchet

After a same-family recurrence:

- capture the failed normal_form_id and route family;
- require a route change at the leverage level;
- prohibit another permit when the route family is unchanged;
- treat cluster-only matches as related evidence, not automatic exclusions;
- fail closed when the store/query is missing.

`no active exclusion` without capture or referenced prior evidence is not clearance after recurrence.

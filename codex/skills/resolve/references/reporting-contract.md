# Reporting Contract

RLR-v4 must report:

- review horizon;
- finding liability;
- normal-form accounting;
- fuse trip and post-trip mutation/net;
- owner-pressure peaks;
- production/test surface;
- negative-ledger captures and leverage changes;
- distillation receipts;
- permit compliance.

The key failure signal is `production_net_after_trip > 0` without a passing distillation/representation warrant.

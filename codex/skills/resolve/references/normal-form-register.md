# Normal-Form Register

A normal form predicts closure of one counterexample family.

A same-family finding after the normal form falsifies it.

Rules:

- one ordinary normal form per family;
- falsification captures negative evidence;
- falsification trips the fuse;
- no second ordinary normal form;
- a later distilled normal form requires RDR-v1 and must change representation, boundary, rule, or ownership.

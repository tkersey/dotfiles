# Holdout Policy

One broad terminal holdout is required after targeted conformance is clean.

The holdout:

- runs against the current pushed tuple;
- is bound to AC/horizon and kernel fingerprints;
- may discover in-horizon, outside-horizon, or contract-invalidating evidence;
- has no direct mutation authority;
- must be sealed as RB-v1.

Terminal closure requires zero novel in-horizon, unknown, or unresolved contract-invalidating CEX.

# `$resolve` and Multi-Plan `$st`

A material `$resolve` campaign that changes code must hold one workspace claim.

Review discovery may remain read-only without a mutation claim.

When `$resolve` changes the selected normal form or resource boundary:

```text
stop mutation
update the plan/kernel
release or amend the claim through `$st`
re-run conflict analysis
obtain a new GCR-v2
```

A review finding in another plan's resource surface is not silently absorbed.
It becomes a cross-plan blocker, contract rebase, or follow-up.
